STAX Equity exhibited at ICSC Las Vegas 2023 — one of the largest commercial real estate conferences in the world — showcasing the firm's analytics-driven approach to net lease retail investment.

## At the Show

The team secured booth 5437R in the South Hall Upper, Level 2 at the Las Vegas Convention Center for the May 21–23 event. Attendees had the opportunity to learn about STAX's analytical approach, view featured properties, and explore partnership opportunities.

## Growth Since Founding

Since its founding in November 2022, STAX Equity deployed more than $100 million in investments, creating over 400 jobs across nine states: Florida, Texas, Arkansas, Tennessee, Alabama, Missouri, Wisconsin, North Carolina, and Kansas.

## The STAX Focus

The firm specializes in acquiring, developing, and selling net-leased retail and industrial properties — including service stations, convenience stores, billboards, and energy infrastructure. Through its real estate division, STAX also leads development efforts for SQRL Holdings, which operates over 160 service station and convenience store locations across 12 states.

Managing Partner Michael Salafia noted the team's excitement about participating and the opportunity to showcase how STAX drives innovation in the CRE marketplace. ICSC Las Vegas remains one of the premier venues for networking, deal-making, and staying ahead of trends in retail real estate.
