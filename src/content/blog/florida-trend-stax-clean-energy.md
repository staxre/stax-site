STAX Real Estate was recognized in Florida Trend's September edition for championing sustainable energy initiatives within the commercial real estate sector — specifically within fuel retail operations.

## Leading the Clean Energy Transition

STAX has emerged as an industry leader in implementing clean energy solutions in the fuel retail industry. The firm has revitalized numerous gas stations and convenience stores, converting them into environmentally responsible facilities that balance profitability with sustainability.

The company continues to lead the way in developing and deploying new clean energy technologies at fuel retail locations — from EV charging infrastructure to solar installations.

## Beyond Fuel Retail

STAX's sustainability efforts extend beyond gas stations. The firm is actively involved in developing solar and wind installations, as well as building green single-tenant commercial structures that meet modern environmental standards.

Ryan Jaama, Vice President at STAX Real Estate, noted the team's enthusiasm about the feature and the firm's continued commitment to environmental stewardship and long-term sustainability goals.

## Why It Matters

The fuel retail industry is at an inflection point. Gas stations that invest in clean energy infrastructure today — EV charging, solar canopies, modernized convenience offerings — will command premium valuations tomorrow. STAX is helping operators and investors position for this transition.
